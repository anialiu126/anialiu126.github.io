## On the Road

Travel has always gone hand in hand with my life in science — conferences,
collaborations, and the occasional adventure in between have taken me across
Europe, Asia, and North America. Wherever I go, I love getting out into the
landscape with a camera.

## Yellowstone

One of my most recent trips was to **Yellowstone National Park** — a landscape
unlike anywhere else I have been, full of geysers, steaming hot springs, and
wide-open wilderness.
